For Developers

You can embed harmlesswebsite on your site! No iframes, just a few elements and one script.

First, add elements where you want harmlesswebsite to be:

<p>Title: <span id="taitl"></span></p>
<div id="baudy">output here...</div>

(note: the title should have id="taitl", and the output window should have id="baudy")

Then, include the script:

<script src="https://harmlesswebsite.leoshi6.repl.co/script.js"></script>

Then, go to a point:

<script>goto(1);</script>

You are encouraged to comply with the licensing.
